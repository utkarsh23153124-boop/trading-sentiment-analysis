# Trading Sentiment Analysis Report

## Methodology

The data was cleaned and preprocessed. Timestamps were converted into a daily format, and the trading dataset was merged with the sentiment dataset (Fear vs Greed). Key metrics such as win rate, average trade size, and trade frequency were calculated for analysis.

---

## Key Insights

1. Traders tend to perform better during Greed periods, with higher average PnL and win rates.
2. Trading activity increases during Greed periods, with more trades and larger position sizes.
3. High-leverage traders show higher risk and volatility, while consistent traders demonstrate more stable performance.

---

## Strategy Recommendations

1. During Fear periods, traders should reduce leverage and position sizes to manage risk.
2. During Greed periods, traders can increase trading activity, but proper risk management is necessary.
3. Focus on consistent traders with higher win rates for more stable returns.
